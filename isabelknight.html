World Bank

Open Data Overview for Managers, Producers, and Users

I created storyboards for a 14-hour set of courses on Open Data for the World Bank to serve as an introductory primer on how leaders around the world can use open data in their businesses. The first course focuses on how managers can promote the use of open data in their businesses, including how to contribute to and benefit from communities of open data users and practitioners. The second course shows data producers how to ensure the data they produce is truly open and accessible to all, including making sure the data they release is properly licensed, well-documented, standardized, anonymized, and machine-readable. The third course shows users how they can find and use open data, make Freedom of Information Act requests for government data, and how they can provide meaningful feedback to data producers to help maintain public datasets.
